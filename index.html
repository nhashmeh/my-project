CV

I am a planetary science graduate student at Tulane University. My primary research
interests involve using ground-based and remote sensing techniques to analyze the
creation, modification, and composition of planetary terrain.

My current project makes use of radar sounding data from ongoing Mars orbiter
missions in order to address questions regarding the nature of subsurface detections
(and lack thereof) within the south polar layered deposits (SPLD). Understanding the
radar behavior of the PLD will aid future efforts in intepreting the paleoclimate
record stored within the ice stratigraphy.
